Group Project

As part of my last year at Anglia Ruskin I worked in a team of 23 people to create this game. I was responsible for making the main gameplay mechanics of this game where the player would interact with points (POIs) of interest around the map. Once activated the POIs would start chalanges for the player to complete such as kill 10 enemies while standing in a specific area. Once all POIs were completed the player could complete the level.

AI Project

This is a side project I worked on after university with the goal of learning how to train an AI system to achieve a goil in a game. The AI would move randomly every frame but it would be rewarded every time its new position is closer to the goal than its last position was. It would also be punished a small amount for each movement it makes which encorages it to find the fastest way to get to the goal
